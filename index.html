places must visit

rishekesh

i had visited in january 2020

The celestial city of Rishikesh is a hermit’s hermitage, a sage’s abode and an adventure lover’s hub. This lively city is among the holiest places of Hindus. The tranquil and sometimes raging river of Ganges flows in eternity in this holy city, providing nourishment and life to many the earthly beings. After having a rendezvous with Rishikesh, the river Ganga leaves the Shivalik hills behind and flows into the plains of northern India.



forest resort - karjat

i had visited in march 2021

At Just 1.5 hours away from Mumbai & Pune, situated on top of a hill with serene views of the green karjat convenient to travel & easy to reach from all modes. Falling in love with supremacy of nature is easy when everything around you is in the rhythm of it. Right from stepping in, you would witness a unique experience. The charming vibes, beautiful & unique trees, the huge meandering pool along with our other infinity pool & amenities with the modern look of the resort will take you to a different aura paired with the perfect hospitality to offer you a pleasant stay experience at one of the best resorts around you. Our other beautiful infinity pool with breathtaking view of the greenery of Karjat is majestic aswell. Dive into the pool while you have the best views of lush green nature’s paradise at one of the unique resort near you.
